Driving Style

Alex's driving style is characterized by exceptional qualifying pace and precision. Known as a "qualifying expert," Alex consistently puts the car on the front row of the grid, setting the tone for race day performance. This expertise is complemented by racecraft and consistency that keeps Redline Racing competitive through the entire season.

"Qualifying is where I establish control. The perfect lap, the perfect setup—that's where the focus lies. From pole position, it's all about converting advantage into results."

Career Highlight

One of Alex Hart's defining moments was taking pole position on debut with Redline Racing. This remarkable achievement announced the driver's arrival at the highest level and set expectations high for the season ahead. The pole position showcased not only raw speed but also the ability to extract maximum performance from the machinery under pressure.

Pole position on debut—a statement of intent that set the tone for the entire campaign.

Role at Redline Racing

As the first driver, Alex carries the responsibility of being the lead architect of performance development. Alex works closely with the engineering team to push the boundaries of what the RRX-25 can achieve, providing feedback that shapes the direction of technical upgrades and setup evolution. Beyond driving, Alex serves as a mentor to the younger drivers in the academy, embodying the philosophy of Redline Racing: "Beyond the Redline."
