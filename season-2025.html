Driver Storyline

Alex Hart remains the calm team leader and qualifying benchmark, but Luca Rossi is closing fast. At just 19, Rossi's confidence is bordering on arrogance, and he has made it clear that he sees himself as a future World Drivers' Champion.

Redline's challenge is keeping both drivers pushing each other without letting the rivalry damage the team. The ideal balance is to use their competition as a performance driver rather than allowing it to become a distraction inside the garage.
